Electronics and Soldering

This week we learned about electronics and how to solder... (it's gonna get textual :P)

First, I learned how some of the common conponent does:

Capacitor is used to hold and store energy. Transistor act as a switch that can transfer energy.

Resistor slow down the the electric flow by applying resistance. LED is a light that transfer chemical energy into heat and light energy (emits light)

Diode control the direction of the flow of electricity. Potentiometer is used to measure the amount electrical current to flowing through it.

Breadboard is a board for making an experimental model of an electric circuit (connected through different points).

Multimeter is used to measure electric current or voltage or resistance of a circuit. Switch is used to connect or cut a circuit.
