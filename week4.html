Week 4

Laser Cutting

This is my favorite week so far! I had been waiting to make my own designed clock for so long.

My design surrounds a girl holding an umbrella background. It was combined with lots of different pictures. Like flower and other symbols.

I learned to used trace bitmap to outline image and use trim to cut out extra line and combine all the images into one piece. The idea behind this is nature and human thoughts on it.

After it is cut by laser printer, I colored it with both spray paint and water color on it. It is a masterpiece :)

I am very satisfied with the result :D


Outline clock original file

Secondly, we made a press fit kit. I made the outline from sketching in Fusion 360 because it is easier to determine the kit's teeth's dimension.

Then I turn it into jpg file and using bitmap and hairline, I turned the image into an outline of my press fit kit.



Thrid, I made a bookmark using laser printing. I found out something really cool that laser cutting aluminum will produce a shiny white surface, it looks so cool :)
